Attractions

SXSW 2024

Address: Zilker Park

The SXSW Music Festival brings together artists, industry professionals, and music lovers from around the world to make connections, elevate their careers, and celebrate the magic of live music from legendary performers to the most exciting new talent.

Bob Bullock Texas State History Museum

Address: 1800 North Congress Avenue

The Bob Bullock Texas State History Museum offers 3 full floors of exhibits featuring everything about Texas also includes a 3D Imax Experience.

Austin Nature & Science Center

Address: 2389 Stratford Dr.

The Austin Nature & Science Center offers many programs for preschoolers, kids, families, and adults with topics ranging from butterflies to caving and much more.

Circuit of the Americas

Address: 9201 Circuit of the Americas Blvd

F1 race track, concerts, plus COTA Karting. Go carts with speeds up to 55mph. Premier karting for all skill levels.

Zilker Botanical Gardens

Address: 2220 Barton Springs Rd.

Tucked away on 26 acres of Austin’s Zilker Metropolitan Park, these gardens are a lush sanctuary of koi ponds, live oaks, and prickly cactuses of all shapes and sizes. They are composed of multiple themed gardens

Pedernales Falls Hiking

Address: 2585 Park Road 6026, Cypress Mill, TX

While the river itself is reason enough to visit the park, scenic trails wind through forests, atop ridges, and along the riverside. Walk the sandy shores of the Pedernales River and climb on the river’s rock formations.

Lady Bird Lake and Boardwalk

Address: Street Prking along S. Lakeshore Blvd.

It wasn't until they finished the boardwalk on Lady Bird Lake that you could circle the entire trail without a few treks on the road. Now there's a full 10.2 mile loop along the water in downtown Austin.

News for Austin

Austin Ranks #5

We ranked 5th as America's most future-ready urban hubs across key metrics representing urban innovation. Austin scored highest in the "business and technology" category covering indicators like STEM/tech jobs, startup, R&D, and patents.

Pitchbook Global Ranking

New ranking places Austin 14th globally and 7th in the U.S. based on overall development (size and maturity) of its venture capital ecosystem.

Standing Out

Austin stands out in ranking of U.S. cities on the tech frontier. The combination of Austin's current concentration leading-edge skills (ranks 5th) and its momentum (ranks 4th) represents a powerful assessment of the competitiveness of its tech workforce.

Exciting Opportunities

Austin is #1 metro where new hires from all generations are welcomed at high rates. Each metro's hires by generation was divided by its respective population by generation. Metros were ranked based on their local population share of generation hires.
