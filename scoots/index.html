Scooters starting at $20/3hr

Scoooters are small and easy to maneuver. You can zip around town and check out the shopping scene, or find a long road and cruise with the wind in your hair. Bring your imagination!

ATVs starting at $50/3hr

A more rugged type of transportation, ATVs will take you down any trail and will give you access to more remote parts of Cozumel. Pack a picnic and get down the trail with exploration as your only to-do list item.

Jeeps starting at $60/3hr

With an expanded capacity for passengers and gear, Jeep rentals expand your exploring options. A Jeep can be used in any weather and still provide rugged off-road capabilities for your adventures.
